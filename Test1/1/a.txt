Maximum product launch delay for profitability?

TotalSales = area do triangulo = (15*3)/2 = 22.5M
TotalCost = Volume * UnitaryCost + NRE = 10000 * 1000 + 2000000 = 12M

TotalSales - TotalCost = 22.5M - 12M = 10.5M

Portanto considerando LostRevenue = 10.5M:

LostRevenue = TotalSales - SalesComDelay

10.5 = 22.5 - ((W-d)^2*H)/2W)
10.5 - 22.5 = - ((15-d)^2)/10


d = 4.05 months

Consluimos que com um delay inferior a 4.05 month ainda temos profit. Caso contrario, TotalSales < TotalCost.
